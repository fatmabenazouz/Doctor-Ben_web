Introduction

General practitioners (GPs) generally treat common illnesses and medical conditions and perform routine checks and examinations to allow you and your family to remain in a good state of health.

Visits to your GP should not happen only when you are not feeling up to scratch or when you are in a bad state of mind. Regular check-ups are recommended, whether you are feeling healthy or not, as your general practitioner focuses on your overall physical and mental health.

Visit your doctor regularly so that they are aware of changes in your health and can address them quickly.
